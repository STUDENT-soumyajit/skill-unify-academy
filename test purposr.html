Skills You Will Master

Reading
  1. Focus on the context to understand the meaning naturally.
  2. Highlight and learn essential vocabulary in each passage.
  3. Read aloud regularly to improve clarity and pronunciation.
Speaking
  1. Engage in regular practice with a partner or group setting.
  2. Prioritize fluency over perfection in early stages of learning.
  3. Record your speech to evaluate tone, clarity, and improvement areas.
Listening
  1. Listen to a variety of content—podcasts, videos, interviews, and talks.
  2. Pay attention to tone, emotion, and the main message being conveyed.
  3. Use subtitles for support at first, then try without them to boost focus.
Writing
  1. Start by writing short, simple paragraphs to express your thoughts.
  2. Ensure clarity, use proper punctuation, and focus on grammar.
  3. Incorporate rich vocabulary to make your writing more vivid and expressive.
